About Me

My journey began one warm summer evening, my mom was headed to the delivery... just kidding I won't go that far back. Who am I? I am an analytical creative who enjoys making digital artwork through the visualizations of data. My curiosity drives my data insights. I see data exploration as a mystery waiting to be solved and I am the key to unlocking the answers. Those answers can bring change. Change that will impact individuals, companies, governments, nations, and possibly even the world. Each of us are required to use our talents to make the world a better place for all. My contribution will be through data.

Discover more about who me through the projects in my portfolio, my blog, LinkedIn profile, and resume. Please don't hesitate to reach out to me with any questions or comments. Enjoy your time here!
